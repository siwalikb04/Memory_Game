HOW TO PLAY

  • Watch and listen: The game will produce a series of tones and lights, each corresponding to a color button. This is the sequence you need to remember.
  • Repeat the sequence: After the game shows you the sequence, it's your turn. Press the color buttons in the same order that the game showed you.
  • Keep up: The game will continue to add more steps to the sequence each time you successfully repeat it. The sequence gets longer and faster as you progress.
  • Don't make a mistake: If you press a wrong button or press them in the wrong order, you lose.
